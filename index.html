Why Gitbook?

Recently I've began to work on something I've been meaning to do for a long time: Bringing my masters thesis online. A full year's transportation research and findings in there has been collecting dust in my hard drive for the past year or so. Even though they maybe old and redundant now, I hope the Design through Research will be useful for anyone that came across this Gitbook.

This GitBook is going to be a work in progress kinda work, as there are gazillions images from my indesign file that needs to be organised better as I publish it bit by bit. In the mean time, you can still read the PDF.
